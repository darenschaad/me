A bit about me

My name is Daren Schaad. I was born in Portland, OR in 1981. I grew up in NE Portland and have lived there most of my life. In 1999 I started college at Oregon State in corvallis, pursuing my bachelor degree in engineering and physics. I spent 6 months in rural Tanzania teaching math and physics to students at a secondary school. I worked as a mechanical engineer for nearly 10 years here in Portland. I owned a business for a brief time period before I encountered some unfortunate circumstances with my business partner, and was forced to discontinue the venture. Now I am enrolled full time in code school at Epicodus in downtown Portland, OR. I am very excited about this newest adventure in my life!
